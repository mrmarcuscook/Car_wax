About Us

Ricky Hall

Founder & CEO

Our Story

ShineTime was founded in 2020 by Ricky Hall, a car enthusiast with over 15 years of experience in the automotive detailing industry. After working for several high-end detailing shops, Ricky noticed a gap in the market for premium waxing services that were both accessible and affordable.

What started as a small operation in Ricky's garage has now grown into one of the most trusted names in car waxing services. Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and exceptional customer service has earned us a loyal customer base and numerous industry awards.

Our Mission

At ShineTime, our mission is to provide the highest quality car waxing services using premium products and techniques, while offering an exceptional customer experience. We believe that every vehicle deserves to look its best, and we're dedicated to making professional-grade waxing services accessible to everyone.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest products and techniques to ensure the best results.
  • Integrity: We're honest and transparent in all our dealings with customers.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve our services and customer experience.
  • Community: We're committed to giving back to the communities we serve.
